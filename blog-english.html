Campus Party Brasil 12 - 2019 - Edition

Hi , okay? Between 2/11/2019 and 2/16/2019 the day I'm writing here, I was able to Campus Party Brazil. Campus Party is the largest technology event in Brazil and one of the largest in the world, bringing together thousands of people to know the main news on the most varied subjects, among them: DevOps, Information Security, IOT (Internet of the Things or Internet of the things), Machine Learning, Entrepreneurship,startups, etc.

Yesterday (02-15-2019) we had a talk with Marcos Pontes, the first Brazilian astronaut and Minister of Science, Technology and Innovation talking about how knowledge and technology can help Brazil's development.Later that same day we also had a talk on Entrepreneurship with Uri Levine, one of the founders of waze. He told us  a little bit about his trajectory until the acquisition by Google.

I was also able to participate in several workshops focused on development Information Security and Entrepreneurship.
